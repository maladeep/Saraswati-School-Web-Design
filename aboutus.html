Welcome to our School

Shree Saraswati Secondary School is co-educational institute located at the heart of Dolpa called Dunai. It offers education from the Secondary level to Bachelor. The school currently has about 1000 students. Our mission is to provide a child-centered learning environment within which every child has an equal opportunity to realize their full potential, have a healthy intellectual, physical and emotional growth and prepare themselves for life in a rapidly changing world. Instead of emphasizing only on academic growth, Saraswati School seeks to provide opportunity for holistic and all-around development of every child through our Complete Schooling Program (CSP).
